A Tribute To Interpol!

Bio:

Interpol is an American rock band from Manhattan, New York, formed in 1997

Having first performed at Luna Lounge along with bands such as the Strokes, Longwave, the National and Stellastarr, Interpol is one of the bands associated with the New York City indie music scene and one of several groups that emerged from the post-punk revival of the 2000s.
